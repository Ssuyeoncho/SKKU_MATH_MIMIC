About MIMIC

MIMIC’s history, core activities, and key achievements at a glance.

SINCE 2018

Our Story

MIMIC began as a student community for studying and discussing mathematics together. Through regular seminars and study sessions each semester, it has grown into a collaborative space where students from diverse majors share mathematical thinking.

ACTIVITIES

What We Do

Each semester, our programs are organized around five key tracks.

  • Seminars
  • Tutoring
  • Paper Reading
  • Student Symposium
  • Study Groups

ACHIEVEMENTS

Recognition

  • Excellent Team, Sungkyun Premium Study Club
  • Outstanding Team, SKKU Tutoring Program
  • Presidential Science Scholarship recipients
  • Prize winners in undergraduate math competitions
