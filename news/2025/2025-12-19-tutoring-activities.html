Tutoring sessions were offered on a regular basis in courses such as Calculus 2, Discrete Mathematics, and Linear Algebra, providing students with steady academic support throughout the term. MIMIC's tutoring is designed not simply to improve grades, but to help students build a deeper understanding of mathematics through guidance from senior students in the department. Tutoring teams formed within MIMIC also took part in the Sungkyun Tutoring Program and received several recognitions, including Best Activity Team (1st overall), Excellent Activity Team (2nd overall), and Best Tutor Award.
