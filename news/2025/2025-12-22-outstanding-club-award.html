On July 17, 20, 24, 27, and 31, as well as August 3 and 7, the club held seven tutoring sessions covering topics from the competition scope, including Linear Algebra. On October 12, 26, 27, and 29, members also held problem-solving study sessions based on past competition problems. Participants first worked through the problems individually, then shared their solutions together, and for problems they could not solve, they continued by discussing them as a group and supplementing their work with textbooks and official solutions. In this way, MIMIC supported members' preparation for the competition through a total of eleven meetings. As a result, MIMIC members achieved meaningful results in the 43rd Korean Collegiate Mathematics Competition, held on November 1, 2025. In Division I, Kook Yesung received a Silver Prize, while Mingook Kim, Jiwoon Ha, and Chaeyoung Han received Bronze Prizes.
